Assessments


Eduvation has designed a unique way of assessments using the potential of psychometric evaluation. Psychometric tests are a standard and scientific method used to measure an individual's mental capabilities and behavioral pattern. Psychometric tests are designed to measure a candidate's suitability for a role based on the required personality characteristics and cognitive abilities. They identify the extent to which the candidate's personality and cognitive abilities match those required to perform the role. This technique has been radically used in hiring and human resource management to ensure the quality and capabilities of hired ones.

Counseling


Our one to one counseling involves scientifically developed aptitude tests and personality tests interpretations that helps us give the best suited career advice to the student based on his/her report of his aptitude and personality. Human capacities are infinite and neither can they be be measured, nor can we judge what one is capable of. Every person has their own unique characteristics, and their own strengths and weaknesses. The key is to identify these unique qualities is through our assessment tests.
